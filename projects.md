# Projects

## Quantum dynamics and laser-matter interaction
Numerical studies of coherent superpositions in Kramers–Henneberger atoms and control of quantum states with laser fields.

## Magnetic field synthesis
Computational modelling of planar inductive coil arrays for low-frequency magnetic field focusing and steering.

## Molecular population dynamics
Simulation of adiabatic and diabatic population trajectories using the Fewest Switches Surface Hopping algorithm.

## Ocean carbon capture
Independent experimental and conceptual work on seawater carbon mineralisation and electrochemical ocean alkalinity enhancement.
