# Chuanben Li

Physics student at University College London, interested in theoretical and computational physics.

<div class="hero">
I am interested in fundamental physics research and in environmentally relevant physical systems. My current and emerging interests include AMO physics, quantum dynamics, soft matter physics, biophysics, and carbon capture related research.
</div>

<div class="highlight-box">
<strong>Current direction:</strong> I hope to pursue master's and PhD research connected to soft matter physics, biophysics, and longer-term fundamental physics questions, with a strong interest in carbon capture related systems and environmental applications.

## Research highlights

- Undergraduate research on **electron dynamics in Kramers–Henneberger atoms** using time-dependent Schrödinger equation simulations
- Group project on **magnetic field synthesis** using computational models of planar inductive coil arrays
- Summer research on **Fewest Switches Surface Hopping (FSSH)** and molecular population dynamics
- Independent work on **ocean carbon capture via electrochemical reactions**
