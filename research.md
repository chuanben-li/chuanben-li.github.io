# Research

## UCL Summer Research Studentship
Worked on electron dynamics in Kramers–Henneberger atoms under laser fields, including numerical solutions of the time-dependent Schrödinger equation and analysis of coherent superpositions.

## UCL Undergraduate Group Project
Studied magnetic field synthesis using planar inductive coil arrays, with Biot–Savart law integration and matrix-based optimisation methods.

## NYU Shanghai Summer Research
Used the Fewest Switches Surface Hopping (FSSH) algorithm in Python to simulate molecular population trajectories and nonadiabatic dynamics.

## Independent Carbon Capture Research
Investigated ocean carbon capture via electrochemical reactions and ocean alkalinity enhancement, including proof-of-concept experimental work on carbonate precipitation.

## Future interests
I hope to develop research at the intersection of:
- fundamental physics
- soft matter physics
- biophysics
- environmentally relevant physical systems
