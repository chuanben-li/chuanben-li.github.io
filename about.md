# About

I am a Physics student at University College London with interests spanning theoretical, computational, and interdisciplinary physics.

My research experience includes:
- AMO and strong-field physics
- computational modelling of magnetic field synthesis
- molecular science and nonadiabatic dynamics simulations
- environmentally motivated work related to ocean carbon capture

I am especially interested in pursuing future master's and PhD research in:
- soft matter physics
- biophysics
- fundamental physics questions connected to complex physical systems
- carbon capture related research from a physics perspective

I am motivated by the idea that rigorous physical theory and computation can contribute both to fundamental understanding and to long-term real-world challenges.
